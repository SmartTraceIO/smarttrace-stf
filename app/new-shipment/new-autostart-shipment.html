Create new shipment with autostart shipment template
Autostart Shipment(s) are based on Autostart Templates, which are assigned to each device in Trackers page.
 
There are two ways to create an Autostart Shipment:
1. Switch on a device
or
​
2. Choose a tracker and hit "Create Autostart Shipment".
Note. The tracker must already be switched on.
